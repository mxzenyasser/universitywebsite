Computer science forms the foundation of our digital age, enabling us to develop software, algorithms, and systems that power our modern world. Study Program

Our curriculum is designed to meet national and international standards, covering a wide range of subjects including pharmacology, medicinal chemistry, pharmaceutics, and clinical pharmacy. The program emphasizes both theoretical knowledge and practical application through advanced laboratory sessions and clinical training.

  • Bachelor of Pharmaceutical Sciences
  • Clinical Pharmacy Specialization
  • Graduate Studies in Pharmacology

Degree Equivalence

The Bachelor of Pharmaceutical Sciences degree from Pharos University is officially recognized and accredited by the Supreme Council of Universities in Egypt. This ensures our graduates are well-prepared for their careers both nationally and internationally.

Quality Assurance Unit

The Quality Assurance Unit is responsible for maintaining and enhancing the quality of education and administrative services within the faculty. We regularly review our programs and processes to ensure they meet the highest standards of excellence.

Compulsory Training Program

The faculty's compulsory training program, or internship, is an essential part of the curriculum. It provides students with hands-on experience in real-world pharmacy settings, including hospitals, community pharmacies, and pharmaceutical companies.
